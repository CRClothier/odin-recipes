Prawn Stir-fry

Description

This recipe is simple, quick and tasty and the prawns mean that it can be a very low calory yet filling option.

Ingredients

  • 150g prawns
  • 1cm ginger
  • 1 bag of stir fried veg
  • 1tbs soy sauce

Instructions

  1. Preheat pan with oil and fry the ginger.
  2. Add veg and cook for fave minutes.
  3. Add a splash of soy sauce.
  4. Add prawns and heat through.
