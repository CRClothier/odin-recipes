Mediterranean Chicken

Description

This Mediterranean mozzarella chicken recipe has been a member favourite on our Online Programme for a long time.

Adding the mozzarella cheese to this low carb chicken dish may seem decadent – but it tastes delicious and keeps you feeling full and satisfied. The secret to this mozzarella chicken tasting so good is in the sauce – making your own tomato based sauce gives this dish a rich intensity that pairs perfectly with the chicken and cheese. With only eight ingredients our Mediterranean mozzarella chicken will soon become a favourite in your house too.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 onion chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, thinly sliced
  • 200g tinned chopped tomatoes
  • Fresh basil, roughly chopped
  • 150mls vegetable stock
  • 125g skinles chiken breast
  • 30g mozzarella cheese sliced

Instructions

    Preheat the oven to fan forced 180°C/200°C/400°F/Gas mark 6.
    Fry onion and garlic for 5 mins. Add chopped tomatoes, basil leaves and stock then bring to the boil. Simmer for 15mins until sauce thickens.
    Place chicken in an oven proof dish. Cover with sauce and add mozzerralla to cover.
    Transfer to oven and cook for 25 mins
