Goan Curry

Description

This recipe is simple, quick and tasty and can be cooked with any protien instead of the chicken.

Ingredients

  • 150g chicken
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 1 bag green beans
  • 1tbs white rice

Instructions

  1. Preheat pan with oil and fry the chicken.
  2. Add veg and cook for five minutes.
  3. Add a splash of coconut milk.
  4. Add other ingredients and heat through.
